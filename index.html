Jobs

AI is being used in Jobs to help humans do Automated Tasks. Many AI chatbots and Virtual Assistant are in Customer Service 24/7, helping out a customer get to a worker. Some AI like Zappyhire and HireVue help in recurting Employees for a company. AI can also assist in medical scans and surgery and some AI robots even take jobs as surgeon. AI also helps the person who is seeking a Job. Websites like Aiaplly help build AI-optimized resume and let the candidate practice interviews with AI mock interview. Like People say "AI is going to take all jobs". That is not 100% true. AI will take jobs. But with help of humans, we can turn AI into a helping guide not a Job taker.
